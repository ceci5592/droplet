Behind the art

The artist Austėja Lašaitė grew up in Lithuania, but now live and work in Copenhagen, Danmark. Doing the day, she studies Production Technology at KEA. Doing the time she's not studying, she works on her brand called Droplet. Droplet is a brand that has been selling paper-cut art since 2017. Austėja challanges traditional art, and constructs her art and products with colourful interpretations of her feelings, of social dilemmas and of political situations. The products are typically different, and belong in the urban environment.
